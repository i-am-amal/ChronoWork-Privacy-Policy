## 1. Introduction
ChronoWork (“the App”) is an offline session and project manager designed to help users track their work and personal tasks efficiently. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

---

## 2. No Data Collection
ChronoWork **does not collect, store, share, or process any personal data**.

- No personal details are collected.
- No analytics or tracking tools are used.
- No information is transmitted to any servers.

Your data stays fully under your control.

---

## 3. Offline-Only App
ChronoWork works entirely **offline**.  
It does not require or use an internet connection for its core features.

---

## 4. Local Device Storage
All your project details, session records, and notes are stored **locally on your device** using **Hive**, a local NoSQL database.

- Your data never leaves your device.
- The developer has *no access* to any user information.
- Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services
ChronoWork does **not** use any third-party cloud services or APIs.

The only external library used is:

- **Hive (offline storage)** — stores all data locally on your device and does not upload anything online.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy
ChronoWork does not collect any data and is safe for users of all ages.  
However, it is not specifically aimed at children.
